Welcome to NeighborGoods

Join our federated sharing platform where neighbors can easily share items, offer services, and build stronger communities. Setting up or joining is simple and intuitive - no technical expertise required.

Key Features

  • Share or borrow items with your local community
  • Offer and request services from neighbors
  • Choose your privacy level (public, local, or private)
  • Simple dispute resolution through community mediation
  • Connect with other neighborhood nodes
